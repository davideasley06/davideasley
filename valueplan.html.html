30 · 60 · 90 · 120 Day Value Plan

A structured, proven approach to delivering impact from day one — balancing rapid discovery with strategic execution across AI, cloud, governance, and executive alignment.

Day 30
LISTEN · LEARN · MAP
Foundation & Discovery
  • Conduct structured listening sessions with CxO, business unit leaders, and technology teams to surface pain points, priorities, and undocumented constraints
  • Audit the current technology estate — application portfolio, cloud footprint, data architecture, and governance maturity — building an honest baseline
  • Map AI and GenAI readiness: identify where capability gaps exist and where quick-win integrations are feasible within existing infrastructure
  • Establish trust with key stakeholders and begin positioning enterprise architecture as a strategic enabler, not a gating function
  • Deliver a concise executive brief: current state assessment, top risks, and an early hypothesis for where the highest-value work lies
Day 60
PRIORITIZE · ALIGN · QUICK WINS
Strategic Alignment & Early Wins
  • Finalize a prioritized transformation roadmap — sequenced by business value, feasibility, and risk — and secure executive alignment before a single dollar is spent
  • Identify and execute 2–3 high-visibility quick wins: cloud cost optimizations, governance bottlenecks removed, or a targeted AI pilot that produces visible results
  • Stand up or strengthen the Architecture Review Board and establish lightweight governance guardrails that enable speed without creating sprawl
  • Launch FinOps baseline review: identify top 20% of cloud spend driving 80% of waste and establish corrective action plans
  • Present a board-ready narrative connecting technology investment to business outcomes, revenue protection, and competitive positioning
Day 90
EXECUTE · BUILD · GOVERN
Transformation in Motion
  • Drive active execution of the highest-priority workstreams — cloud modernization, application rationalization, or GenAI integration — with clear owners and measurable milestones
  • Establish a Cloud Center of Excellence (CCoE) with embedded FinOps governance, Communities of Practice, and architectural standards that scale across teams
  • Launch the enterprise AI strategy: defined use cases, data readiness requirements, governance guardrails, and a phased integration plan aligned to business priorities
  • Reduce architecture approval cycle times through Agile-based governance — targeting a 25–30% improvement in decision velocity within the quarter
  • Deliver first measurable ROI milestone — cost savings realized, delivery speed improved, or a production AI capability shipped
Day 120
SCALE · SUSTAIN · LEAD
Durable Competitive Advantage
  • Scale successful pilots into enterprise-wide programs — expanding AI integrations, FinOps disciplines, and modernization patterns across all business units
  • Institutionalize a culture of architectural accountability: governance frameworks, decision rights, and technology standards that operate independently of any single leader
  • Deliver a comprehensive 12-month technology strategy to the board — with defined KPIs, investment rationale, and a clear line-of-sight from technology decisions to EBITDA impact
  • Establish the organization as an AI-ready, cloud-optimized enterprise — with the governance maturity, talent capability, and architectural foundation to sustain transformation at speed
  • Position technology as a board-level competitive differentiator — not a cost to be managed, but a compounding asset that drives long-term enterprise value
